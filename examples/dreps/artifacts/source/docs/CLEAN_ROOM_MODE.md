# Clean Room Mode

The public DREPS export is designed to be clean-room friendly.

## Principles

- Export only evidence needed to understand the lab.
- Avoid secrets and credentials.
- Hash artifacts with SHA-256.
- Keep findings educational and non-destructive.
- Keep premium scoring and certification outside the public lab.

## Why it matters

Icare Sentinel Lab can serve as a public case study without exposing private Doctrine Platform internals.
